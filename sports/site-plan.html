Purpose

This website would include sports teams in every major American sport. It will include the NFL, NBA, MLB, and NHL. Each team with-in the website will show their location, and team website. The website will also show individual cities. Find your hometown and all the info on your teams will be there.

Audience

This is for people who want to see their major sports teams around them, and possibly choose who to pick as your team.
